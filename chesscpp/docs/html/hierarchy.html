 CCastleA Castle is a special Move that moves the King by two fields and moves the Rook to the position the King has transitioned through. This special Move requires additional constraints, among such that the King does not transit through a check and that the path between King and Rook is free. Additionally, this Move is only possible if the King and Rook have not yet moved. The latter is configured in the BoardState
